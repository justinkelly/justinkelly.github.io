--- layout: post title: Simple Invoices for Windows published: true date: 2010-09-15 tags: - simple invoices --- I'm pleased to announce the availability of Simple Invoices for Windows.  This is the simplest way to get Simple Invoices running locally on a Windows PC.  Just download, unzip, double click SimpleInvoices.exe and thats it.

Nothing to install - it just works from where ever you unzip it
- so can run from usb etc.. fine

Thanks so much to John from http://teammc.cc/ for creating this and agreeing to maintain it - so future releases of Simple Invoices will be available as the normal webapp and for windows.

Download:
http://code.google.com/p/simpleinvoices/downloads/detail?name=simpleinvoices_for_windows.2010.2.update-1.zip

Post on the forum ( http://simpleinvoices.org/forum ) it you have any issues or queries
